# PhageDB Knowledge Base Launched

Welcome to the PhageDB Knowledge Base - a comprehensive guide to bacteriophage database resources!

## What We Offer

- **14+ Database Reviews**: In-depth assessments of major phage databases
- **Quality Ratings**: Honest evaluations based on real researcher experience
- **Use Case Guides**: Practical advice for common research workflows
- **Complete Catalog**: Tracking 35+ databases containing phage data

## Current Coverage

### Primary Databases Reviewed:
- BFVD - AlphaFold structures
- UniProt - Protein annotations
- VOGDB - Viral protein families
- NCBI Viral Genomes
- IMG/VR - Metagenomic viruses
- Pfam - Protein domains
- PDB - Experimental structures
- InPhared - Consistent annotations
- PHASTER - Prophage finder
- MillardLab - Host range data
- PhagesDB - Actinobacteriophages
- Pharokka - Annotation tool
- Prophage-DB - 356K prophages
- PhageScope - Aggregator
